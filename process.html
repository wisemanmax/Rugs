THE PROCESS

Every Rugs By Quana piece begins with a story and ends with a soulful, hand-finished heirloom. From sourcing fibers to the final shearing, each step is guided by careful craftsmanship, warm texture, and a commitment to timeless design.

From Sketch to Studio

It starts with a conversation about color, mood, and movement. We sketch your vision, gather inspiration from architecture and textiles, and translate the feeling of your space into a tactile blueprint.

Each design is mapped knot by knot, balancing depth, dimension, and flow. The goal is a rug that feels alive â€” a piece that anchors the room while reflecting the quiet artistry of the maker.

How Your Rug Is Made

01

Material Selection

We source premium New Zealand wool and organic cotton foundations for exceptional softness, resilience, and rich color depth.

02

Hand Dyeing

Fibers are hand dyed in small batches, creating layered hues that glow under natural light and stay vibrant for years.

03

Tufting & Carving

Each line is tufted by hand, then sculpted and carved to create sculptural contours and a signature plush feel.

04

Finishing Touches

Edges are bound, the surface is sheared for an even finish, and every rug is inspected for durability before it leaves the studio.

Signature Materials

The beauty of a Rugs By Quana piece comes from the materials beneath your feet. Every fiber is chosen for how it feels, how it wears, and how it holds color.

New Zealand Wool

Silky, springy, and naturally stain-resistant, this wool delivers a cloud-like pile with exceptional longevity.

Organic Cotton

A breathable foundation that keeps each rug structured, stable, and smooth against the floor.

Botanical Dyes

Gentle, layered pigments inspired by earth and sea tones provide a nuanced palette that feels modern yet grounded.

Crafted to Feel Like Home

Your rug is more than a decorative piece. It is a tactile landscape made to welcome you home, soften every step, and hold the memory of slow, intentional craft.
