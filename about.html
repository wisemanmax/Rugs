THE ARTISAN

Crafting Contemporary Floor Art Since 2018

Where Tradition Meets Modern Design

I'm Artem, a textile artist specializing in contemporary rug design. My journey began in a small workshop where I learned traditional hand-tufting techniques from master craftsmen. What started as an apprenticeship evolved into a passion for reimagining what rugs could be in modern spaces.

Each piece I create is a dialogue between heritage craft and contemporary aesthetics. I work exclusively with premium natural materials, primarily wool and silk, sourced from ethical suppliers. The designs emerge from my fascination with minimalism, architecture, and the subtle interplay of texture and color.

Every rug is made to order, ensuring that each client receives a unique piece tailored to their space. From the initial concept to the final knot, I oversee every step of the creation process in my studio.

Design Philosophy

Timeless Design

I create pieces that transcend trends, focusing on fundamental principles of composition, balance, and restraint. Each rug is designed to remain relevant for decades, not seasons.

Honest Materials

Only natural fibers make it into my work. Wool, silk, and cotton are chosen for their beauty, durability, and sustainability. No synthetics, no compromises.

Human Craft

Every rug is made entirely by hand using traditional techniques. This slow, deliberate process ensures quality and creates unique character that machines cannot replicate.
